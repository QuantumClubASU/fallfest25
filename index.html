About IBM Fest 2025

Hosted by the Quantum Club @ ASU, IBM Fest 2025 is a premier event bringing together students, researchers, and industry professionals to explore the cutting edge of quantum computing and artificial intelligence.

Whether you're a curious beginner or an advanced quantum explorer, join us for workshops, keynote speeches, hands-on labs, and networking opportunities with IBM experts and the ASU quantum community.
